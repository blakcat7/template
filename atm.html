Circular Kinetic Ceiling - Arabian Travel Market (Atlantis)

Fractal Systems presents you the most innovative kinetic systems for art installations, interiors, clubs, shows, concerts, exhibitions and corporate events.

Fractal Systems' Kinetic Ceiling consists of controlled LED lights elements for synchronized and precision-guided winches, configurable according to your desire, capable of creating amazing animations spatial LED elements.

This particular Kinetic Ceiling was manufactured, designed and delivered by Fractal Systems for Atlantis exhibiting at ATM 2017 in Dubai World Trade Centre.

Using very thin, specially designed, signal cable the motor unit can move the objects up and down with precision. Each circle can be adjusted individually in height, speed, color and luminance. Bespoke array creations of controllable motor units with light balls can generate complex three-dimensional abstract shapes and visual effects.
