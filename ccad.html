Kinetic Ceiling - CityScape Abu Dhabi 2017 (Department of Municipal Affairs and Transport)

This particular Kinetic Ceiling was manufactured, designed and delivered by Fractal Systems for CityScape Abu Dhabi 2017 in ADNEC.

Using very thin, specially designed, signal cable the motor unit can move the objects up and down with precision. Each light ball can be adjusted individually in height, speed, color and luminance. Bespoke array creations of controllable motor units with light balls can generate complex three-dimensional abstract shapes and visual effects.
